Analysis¶

We analyzed our data using a few methods. The first method is the plotting of how relative entropy changes with respect to changes in different parameters.

Relative Entropy¶

In [1]:
import analysis
analysis.rel_icpc()
In [2]:
analysis.rel_ml()
In [3]:
analysis.rel_sc()

As can be seen, an increase in the information content per column results in better results as evidenced by the lower absolute relative entropy.

Sites Overlap¶

In [4]:
analysis.sites_icpc()
In [5]:
analysis.sites_ml()
In [6]:
analysis.sites_sc()

An increase in informational content results in an increase in sites overlap. Changes in motif length do not seem to affect the amount of sites overlap. Increasing sequence count seems to increase site overlap, but the effect is unclear.

Conclusion¶

Increasing the informational content seems to have the best result. Finer graduations are required in order to draw better conclusions.
